About Me



I'm 23 years old. I graduated with honors from Indiana State University in May 2018 with a bachelor's degree in Biology. While trying to figure out what to do next, I decided to look into learning to code, which I quickly discovered I enjoyed. Currently, I'm a student at ElevenFifty Academy in Fishers, IN, where I'm learning to use JavaScript, Python, Django, HTML, CSS, TypeScript, Angular, and Flask. I'm also proficient with using microsoft office, github, and the Windows terminal.
In addition to coding, I enjoy playing video games, reading, writing stories, and drawing.
I'm now looking for a career in which I can be challenged, continue to learn, and put my creativity to good use.
